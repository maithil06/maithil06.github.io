Wardrobe Fashion Comparer

This is an AI-powered wardrobe assistant that matches your clothes to current fashion trends by analyzing photos you take. It uses computer vision to create image embeddings, stores them in FAISS for lightning-fast similarity search, and applies K-means clustering to group similar fashion styles together. Search Groceries with Ingredients

This is a grocery search engine that lets you find products by their specific ingredients using natural language queries. It creates text embeddings from ingredient lists and product descriptions, stores them in FAISS for super-fast vector search, and matches your ingredient requirements against thousands of grocery items in milliseconds. Built to handle queries like "find snacks without nuts and dairy" or "pasta with whole wheat and low sodium" by computing semantic similarity between your search and actual product ingredients
